Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

Overview

BLE Insight ("the App") is a Bluetooth Low Energy development tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information.

Data Collection

BLE Insight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processed by the App remains entirely on your device.

Bluetooth Data

The App accesses Bluetooth hardware to scan for and communicate with nearby BLE devices. This includes:

  • Device names and identifiers of nearby BLE devices
  • Advertisement data broadcast by BLE devices
  • RSSI (signal strength) values
  • Service and characteristic data from connected devices

This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Location Data

The App may request location permission solely for iBeacon detection functionality. Location data is used only within the App and is never collected, stored, or shared externally.

Session History

Communication sessions and test scripts are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. You can delete this data at any time from within the App.

Third-Party Services

BLE Insight does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Export

The App provides CSV and JSON export functionality. Exported files are created locally and shared only through your explicit action via the iOS share sheet.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please leave a review or comment on the App Store. We read all feedback and respond as needed.
